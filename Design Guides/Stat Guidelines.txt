Stat Guidelines

Poor	7-8	A noticeable weakness.
Below Average	9-10	Functional, but not a strength.
Good	11-12	Better than most.
Excellent	13-14	A defining strength.
Exceptional	15-16	Rare in the starting roster.
Heroic	17-18	Avoid at the start. Earned through progression.

I'd actually make 15-16 the ceiling for Adventure 0. That leaves plenty of room for level-ups, equipment, and meta unlocks to matter.
